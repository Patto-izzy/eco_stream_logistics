# Investigation & Analysis

## Competitor analysis

- DHL (example)
  - What works: clear pricing for business customers, professional photography, strong trust signals.
  - What doesn’t work for our brief: global brand sites are complex and not focused on local, small-business UX.

- Local courier (example)
  - What works: easy-to-find contact/quote forms and clear local rates.
  - What doesn’t work: many local sites lack accessibility, low contrast, and poor mobile layouts.

## User needs

- Quickly find pricing and delivery options.
- Request a quote with minimal friction (simple form).
- See proof of environmental commitment (electric fleet mentioned visually).
- Trust signals: local focus, contact details and clear prices.
